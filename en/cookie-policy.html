1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, tablet, or smartphone) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, provide a better browsing experience, and supply information to website owners.

Cookies can be "first-party" (set by the website you are visiting) or "third-party" (set by a different domain). They can also be "session" cookies (deleted when you close your browser) or "persistent" cookies (stored on your device until they expire or you delete them).

2. How We Obtain Consent

When you first visit alkosafe.com, you will see a cookie consent banner that provides you with three options:

  • Accept All Cookies -- Enables all cookie categories, including analytics, marketing, and functional cookies.
  • Reject Non-Essential Cookies -- Only strictly necessary cookies are placed on your device. Analytics, marketing, and functional cookies are blocked.
  • Manage Preferences -- Opens a detailed panel where you can enable or disable each cookie category individually.

Your consent preferences are stored for 12 months. After this period, or if you clear your cookies, you will be asked to provide consent again. You can change your preferences at any time by clicking the cookie settings link in our website footer.

4. Google Consent Mode v2

AlkoSafe implements Google Consent Mode v2, which adjusts the behaviour of Google tags (Analytics, Ads) based on your cookie consent preferences. When you have not consented to analytics or marketing cookies:

  • Google Analytics sends cookieless pings instead of setting cookies, preserving basic aggregate measurement without identifying individual users.
  • Google Ads conversion tracking operates in a modelled manner, using privacy-safe signals to estimate conversions without reading or writing advertising cookies.
  • Once you grant consent, full cookie-based tracking resumes according to standard Google tag behaviour.

This approach ensures compliance with GDPR, KVKK, and the EU ePrivacy Directive while allowing us to maintain a minimum level of measurement accuracy.

6. How to Control Cookies

Via Our Cookie Banner

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time by clicking the cookie settings link in our website footer. This will re-open the consent banner where you can update your choices.

Via Your Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. Instructions for popular browsers:

  • Google Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies and other site data
  • Apple Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
  • Mozilla Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings > Cookies and Site Permissions > Manage and delete cookies

Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of alkosafe.com, particularly features related to your shopping cart and checkout.
