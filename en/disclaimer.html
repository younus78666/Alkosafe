1. AlkoSafe is a Screening Tool

Critical Notice

AlkoSafe is a personal screening tool only and is NOT a diagnostic device. AlkoSafe is not intended to:

  • Diagnose any medical condition or alcohol-related disorder
  • Provide a legally admissible Blood Alcohol Concentration (BAC) measurement
  • Replace official breathalyser, blood, or urine testing conducted by law enforcement or medical professionals
  • Serve as clinical evidence in any medical, legal, or employment proceeding

AlkoSafe provides an indication of the possible presence of alcohol in saliva. It is designed to assist individuals in making personal decisions and should never be treated as a definitive or legally binding result.

2. No Reliance for Legal Purposes

Legal Warning

A negative AlkoSafe result does not mean your Blood Alcohol Concentration (BAC) is zero. The absence of a colour change on the test strip does not guarantee that you are below the legal driving limit in your jurisdiction.

An AlkoSafe result cannot be used to challenge, dispute, or override the results of a police breathalyser test, blood test, or any other officially administered alcohol test. Courts and law enforcement agencies do not recognise saliva screening strips as evidential testing devices.

3. Accuracy Limitations

AlkoSafe has been independently tested and validated with the following performance characteristics:

Metric Value
Sensitivity (true positive rate) 99.7%
Specificity (true negative rate) 99.5%
Approximate false negative rate ~0.3%
Approximate false positive rate ~0.5%

These figures represent performance under controlled laboratory conditions. Real-world accuracy may be affected by several factors, including but not limited to:

  • Insufficient wait time — you must wait at least 15 minutes after eating, drinking, or smoking before testing
  • Food residue in the mouth at the time of testing
  • Mouthwash or oral hygiene products containing alcohol
  • Dry mouth or insufficient saliva production
  • Improper storage — exposure to extreme heat, cold, or humidity

Users should always follow the instructions provided with the product to ensure the most reliable results.
