4. KVKK Notice for Turkish Residents

If you are a resident of Turkey, the Turkish Personal Data Protection Law (KVKK, Law No. 6698) grants you the following rights:

  1. The right to learn whether your personal data is being processed.
  2. The right to request information about the processing of your personal data.
  3. The right to learn the purpose of processing and whether your data is used in accordance with that purpose.
  4. The right to know the third parties to whom your personal data is transferred, domestically or abroad.
  5. The right to request correction of incomplete or inaccurate personal data.
  6. The right to request deletion or destruction of your personal data under the conditions set out in Article 7 of the KVKK.
  7. The right to object to any result that arises against you through the analysis of your processed data exclusively by automated systems.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at info@alkosafe.com.
