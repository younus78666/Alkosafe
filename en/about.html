About AlkoSafe

Turkish Manufacturing. European Standards.

AlkoSafe is manufactured by CROM TEST, a Turkish company specialising in rapid chromatographic immunoassay diagnostic test production. We make fast, reliable, and affordable testing products for individuals, workplaces, and institutions — built to international quality standards with premium raw materials sourced from Europe's leading laboratory suppliers.

Our Story

Who We Are

CROM TEST was established to address a clear gap in the Turkish and regional rapid test market: products that combine the quality of Western European manufacturers with the pricing accessibility and domestic manufacturing advantages of Turkish production.

The result is AlkoSafe — a saliva alcohol test that uses the same materials as European hospital labs, validated with Swiss and British research methodology, manufactured in Turkey under ISO 13485:2016 quality management.

We operate under the EGEFE & Green Med group — a company with over 20 years of experience across hospital systems, forensic applications, law enforcement equipment, and large-scale healthcare infrastructure.

Production

Manufacturing in Turkey

AlkoSafe is designed and manufactured entirely in Turkey. Our production facility operates under ISO 13485:2016 quality management — the international standard for medical device manufacturing — with full traceability from raw material intake to finished product release.

R&D

Swiss Materials. British Know-How.

The core of AlkoSafe's performance is its membrane. We use nitrocellulose membranes sourced exclusively from Merck (Germany) and Sartorius (Switzerland) — the same suppliers used by European hospital diagnostic laboratories.

Our research methodology — the formulation of the Alcohol Oxidase enzyme system, the buffer chemistry, and the threshold calibration — was developed in collaboration with UK and Swiss technical partners.
