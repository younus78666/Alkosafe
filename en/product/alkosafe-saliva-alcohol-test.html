About the Product

What is AlkoSafe?

AlkoSafe is a saliva alcohol test strip designed for fast, reliable alcohol screening at home, in the workplace, or before driving. Using enzymatic detection technology, AlkoSafe identifies the presence of alcohol in saliva at concentrations from 0.2 promil (0.02% BAC) — the legal driving limit in Turkey and across much of Europe — delivering a clear result in just 2 minutes.

Unlike digital breathalysers that require calibration, batteries, and regular maintenance, AlkoSafe requires nothing more than the test strip itself. There are no moving parts, no charging, and no setup. Each strip is individually sealed in a foil pouch with a 24-month shelf life at room temperature, making it practical for everyday carry, workplace first-aid kits, and vehicle glove compartments.

AlkoSafe is manufactured in Turkey under ISO 13485:2016 quality management standards — the international benchmark for medical device manufacturing. The test uses nitrocellulose membranes sourced exclusively from Merck and Sartorius, the same Swiss and German suppliers used in European hospital diagnostic laboratories.

In clinical validation studies, AlkoSafe demonstrated a sensitivity of 99.7% and a specificity of 99.5% for alcohol detection. The test uses an Alcohol Oxidase enzymatic cascade reaction, the same core technology used in professional alcohol screening equipment, adapted into a single-use, portable format.

How to Use

How to Use AlkoSafe

Using AlkoSafe takes under 3 minutes. Wait 15 minutes after eating, drinking, or smoking before testing.

1

Prepare

Wait at least 15 minutes after eating, drinking, or smoking. Open the foil pouch and remove the strip immediately before use.

2

Test

Place the absorbent end under your tongue. Hold in place for exactly 2 minutes. Do not chew or move the strip.

3

Read the Result

No colour change = no alcohol detected above threshold. Blue or purple = alcohol detected at or above 0.2 promil.

ObservationInterpretation
No colour change Alcohol not detected above 0.2 promil
Faint blue / purple Borderline — retest after 15 minutes
Clear blue / purple Alcohol detected — do not drive

+
AlkoSafe has a clinical sensitivity of 99.7% and specificity of 99.5%. For a personal screening tool these are strong figures. AlkoSafe is a screening device — for legally admissible results, official breathalyser or blood tests by authorities are required.

+
AlkoSafe detects alcohol for as long as it remains above 0.2 promil in your system. The body eliminates alcohol at approximately 0.1 promil per hour.
