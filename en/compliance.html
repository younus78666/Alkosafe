AlkoSafe Certifications & Quality Standards

AlkoSafe is manufactured to international quality standards backed by independent certification. This page explains what each certification means, why it matters for a product you rely on for personal safety decisions, and how to verify our claims.

Certification

CE Mark — European Conformity

The CE Mark is the manufacturer's declaration that AlkoSafe meets the health, safety, and environmental protection requirements of applicable European directives. CE marking allows a product to be placed on the market in European Union countries and countries that recognise European standards — including Turkey.

For AlkoSafe, CE marking means the product has been evaluated for safety and performance, the manufacturing processes meet European standards, and technical documentation is maintained and available for regulatory inspection.

CE marking is not a quality endorsement from a third party — it is a self-declaration by the manufacturer backed by technical documentation. It creates legal accountability: if a CE-marked product causes harm due to non-compliance, the manufacturer bears full legal responsibility.

CE Certified

What CE Mark means for you

  • Product assessed against European safety and performance standards
  • Technical documentation exists and is maintained
  • Manufacturer accepts legal accountability

  • Regulatory authorities can request inspection at any time
Quality Standard

ISO 13485:2016 — Medical Device Quality Management

ISO 13485:2016 is the international standard for quality management systems in medical device manufacturing. It is widely recognised as the most demanding quality framework applicable to device manufacturers — more stringent than general ISO 9001.

An ISO 13485:2016 certified manufacturer has demonstrated that their quality management system consistently meets customer requirements and applicable regulatory requirements. For CROM TEST — the manufacturer of AlkoSafe — this means every stage of the production process operates under documented, audited quality controls.

What ISO 13485 means in practice

Batch Quality Controls

Every production batch manufactured under documented controls

Qualified Suppliers

Raw material suppliers (Merck, Sartorius) are qualified and monitored

Full Traceability

Every batch traced from raw material to final product

Non-Conformance Management

Issues recorded, investigated, and corrected

External Audits

Quality system audited by an accredited external body

Post-Market Surveillance

Ongoing monitoring of product performance

Materials

Swiss & German-Grade Raw Materials

AlkoSafe's performance is directly dependent on the quality of its core materials. The nitrocellulose membrane — the functional heart of the test — is sourced exclusively from Merck and Sartorius, two of the world's leading suppliers headquartered in Germany and Switzerland respectively.

These are the same suppliers whose materials are used in hospital diagnostic laboratories across Europe. The choice of premium membrane material directly impacts sensitivity, specificity, and consistency across production batches.
